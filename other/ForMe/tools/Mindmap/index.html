Create a new idea

Click, hold, and drag from an existing idea, then release.

Move an idea

Click, hold, and wait, then drag and release.

To remove an idea

Mouse over and click the close button to remove.

To edit an idea

Mouse over, click once, and type to edit.

To link two ideas

Click, hold, and drag to another idea. Wait until you see the link icon, then release.

To unlink two ideas

Mouse over the connection until you see the scissors, then click to unlink.

Panning the canvas

To pan, click the canvas, hold, and drag.
